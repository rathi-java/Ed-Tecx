Examination Instructions

Please read all the instructions below carefully before you begin the test. Failure to follow these guidelines may lead to disqualification or invalidation of your test.

General Guidelines

  • Ensure that your internet connection is stable throughout the duration of the test to avoid disruptions.
  • Do not refresh, close, or navigate away from the test window as this may result in the loss of your progress.

Time Limit

The test is strictly time-bound, and you will have a total duration of 60 minutes to complete all sections. Please ensure that you manage your time effectively and submit your responses before the deadline.

  • Once the timer reaches zero, the test will be automatically submitted.

Test Format

  • The test comprises different types of questions, including multiple-choice questions (MCQs), coding challenges, and descriptive written answers.
  • Some sections may have negative marking, so answer carefully and avoid guessing if unsure.
  • Ensure that you fully understand the question before selecting or submitting an answer.

Submission Guidelines

  • Click the "Submit Test" button once you have completed all questions.
  • Review all your answers before final submission, as you will not be allowed to modify them afterward.
  • Partial submissions are allowed, but once the test is submitted, it cannot be reopened.

Technical Requirements

  • For the best experience, use the latest version of a supported web browser such as Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, or Microsoft Edge.
  • Disable pop-up blockers to ensure all questions and prompts are displayed correctly.
  • Using a desktop or laptop is recommended over mobile devices for a smooth test-taking experience.

Rules & Violations

  • Strict monitoring is in place, and any form of cheating or suspicious behavior will lead to disqualification.
  • Sharing test questions, screenshots, or answers with others is strictly prohibited and may result in legal action.
  • Any attempt to manipulate the test platform or violate exam policies will be considered malpractice and will lead to severe consequences.

Support

If you face any technical difficulties, immediately contact the support team for assistance. Avoid closing the test window while waiting for help.

  • Email: support@example.com
  • If you are disconnected, try to reconnect within 5 minutes, or inform the support team.
  • Technical assistance is available throughout the test duration for critical issues only.
