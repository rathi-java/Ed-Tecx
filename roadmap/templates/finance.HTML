Finance Roadmap
Phase 1: Understanding Financial Fundamentals
  • Basic Accounting Concepts – Assets, liabilities, equity, revenue, expenses.
  • Financial Statements – Balance sheet, income statement, cash flow statement.
  • Time Value of Money – Present value, future value, compounding, discounting.
Phase 2: Corporate Finance
  • Capital Budgeting – Investment decisions, NPV, IRR.
  • Cost of Capital – Weighted Average Cost of Capital (WACC).
  • Risk Management – Hedging, portfolio diversification.
Phase 3: Financial Markets & Investments
  • Stock Market Basics – Stocks, bonds, derivatives.
  • Investment Analysis – Fundamental vs. Technical Analysis.
  • Portfolio Management – Asset allocation, risk-return tradeoff.
Phase 4: Advanced Financial Concepts
  • Financial Modeling – Excel-based financial projections.
  • Mergers & Acquisitions – Valuation, due diligence.
  • FinTech & Blockchain – Digital payments, cryptocurrencies.
