Information & Technology FAQs

  • What is Information Technology (IT)?

    IT is the use of computers, networking, storage, and other technologies to process and store data.

  • What are the main branches of IT?

    Major branches include software development, cybersecurity, networking, data science, and IT support.

  • What is the difference between hardware and software?

    Hardware is the physical components of a computer, while software is the set of instructions that tell the hardware what to do.

  • What is an operating system (OS)?

    An OS is system software that manages computer hardware, software resources, and provides common services for applications.

Management FAQs

  • What is management?

    Management is the process of planning, organizing, leading, and controlling resources to achieve specific goals.

  • What are the main functions of management?

    Planning, organizing, leading, and controlling are the core functions of management.

  • What are the different levels of management?

    There are three levels: top-level, middle-level, and operational-level management.

  • What is project management?

    Project management involves planning, executing, and monitoring a project to meet specific goals within a given timeframe.
