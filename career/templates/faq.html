Frequently Asked Questions

What is Reader Club, and how does it work?

+

ReaderClub is an online skill development platform that offers study materials related to every field, certification, internship and job opportunities.

How can I apply for internships and jobs through this platform?

+

You can browse job listings, find oportunities that match your skills and preferences and submit application through job dashboard.

Do I need a membership for access to study materials and practice questions?

+

Basic content is free, but premium materials and practice tests may require a subscription.

What courses and skills does this platform offer?

+

The platform covers technology, business, soft skills, and industry specific courses to help students with career development.

Are the internships on the platform paid or unpaid?

+

We offer a mix of paid and unpaid internships. Each listing clearly mentions the details, including stipend, roles and requirement.
